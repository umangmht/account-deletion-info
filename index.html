Request Account Deletion

This page allows users of the GK Quiz: Play Solo or Challenge app to request account and data deletion.

How to Request Deletion

Please email us at developer.umang.mhta@gmail.com with your account email or user ID and subject: “Delete My GK Quiz Account”. We will process your request within 7 days.

What Will Be Deleted?

  • Your profile, stats, XP, and leaderboard info
  • Any saved game progress
  • Your Firebase authentication account

Data Retention

Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered. We may retain logs for up to 30 days for fraud prevention and legal compliance.

For more details, see our Privacy Policy.
