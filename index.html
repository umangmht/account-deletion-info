Request Account Deletion

This page allows users of the QuizApp app to request account and data deletion.

How to request deletion

Please email us at ydeveloper.umang.mhta@gmail.com with your account email and subject: “Delete My QuizApp Account”.

What will be deleted?

  • Your profile, stats, XP, and leaderboard info
  • Any saved game progress
  • Your Firebase authentication account

Data retention

Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered. We may retain logs for up to 30 days for fraud prevention and legal compliance.
